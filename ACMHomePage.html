ABOUT US

The largest and most established international scientific and industrial computer society is the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM).

The BPHC chapter of ACM is a vibrant community of like minded people, who are enthusiastic about Computer Science.

The chapter supports and coordinates a wide range of activities, including hashcode, GSoC, Projects(intraclub as well as under profs), guidance for hackathons, SI and thesis talks. Additionally, we have SIGs (Special Interest Groups) in a number of topics where enthusiasts gather, communicate, and collaborate on projects specific to those domains.
